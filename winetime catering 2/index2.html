Healthy

We are conscious that as a catering company we can have a deep impact on our environment: first by serving quality food sourced from minded vendors & made with the freshest ingredients. Secondly, by creating the most satisfying ordering experience and making your satisfaction the center of what we do. Our mission is to share the best nutrition practices with social-economical groups that need it the most. We believe in the adage that "we are what we eat" and the power of a good meal to bring people together.

Gourmet

We pride ourselves on serving gourmet meals at affordable prices. Our cuisine is heavily influenced by our international backgrounds and all our adventures. As food artists, we hope to feed your senses with a different experience at each meal. Our team has over 20 years of experience in hospitality and as restaurant chain owners for the last 10 years, we understand your needs and what makes a catering a gourmet experience.

Always fresh

Our ingredients are sourced as locally as possible. We pride ourselves on serving natural products of higher grades and we carefully select all our vendors. Each order is prepared as close as possible to the delivery time so the food will always arrive fresh. Our menus evolve every season and we constantly working on new recipes to delight your palette.
